📋 Before You Begin — Please Read

Purpose of This Survey Thank you for participating. The objective of this survey is to assess the safety culture within our organisation across the four pillars of the Safety Management System (SMS) as defined by ICAO Annex 19. Your insights are vital to identifying systemic strengths and areas for improvement that data alone cannot capture.
🤝
Voluntary Participation You may skip any question or stop the survey at any time. There is no obligation to complete every item.
🔒
Data Handling & Confidentiality All responses are stored securely. Results are reported only in aggregate form — for example, "70% of respondents across the organisation..." — ensuring no individual can be identified by their specific answers.
📊
About the Demographic Questions Questions regarding your role and department appear at the end of this survey. They are used strictly for high-level trend analysis and are entirely optional.
✈️ A Note on Just Culture We are seeking your most candid perspectives. In the spirit of Just Culture, there are no "right" or "wrong" answers — only your honest experience. Your input directly contributes to the strengthening of our Safety Management System.
